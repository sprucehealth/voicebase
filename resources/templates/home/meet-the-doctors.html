Doctors on Spruce are top, board-certified dermatologists from across the United States.

Get to know just a few of them.

Dr. Elan Newman
BOARD-CERTIFIED DERMATOLOGIST
Dr. Elan Newman studied medicine and completed his residency at the University of California, San Diego. Dr. Newman practices at the Sharp Rees-Stealy Medical Group in Chula Vista, California. He is a fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology.
“People will finally be able to have their skin conditions assessed and treated by a real dermatologist, using little more than their smartphone, an internet connection, and a couple of selfies!”
Dr. Rishi Patel
BOARD-CERTIFIED DERMATOLOGIST
Dr. Patel is a member of the American Academy of Dermatology and the American Society of Dermatopathology. He earned his medical degree from Case Western Reserve University and completed his dermatology residency at University Hospitals Case Medical Center. As an Assistant Professor in Dermatology and Pathology at NYU, Dr. Patel was awarded the Full Time Faculty of the Year Award in 2010 and was voted to New York Times Magazine’s Super Doctors® Rising Stars.
“I am excited to be a part of such an innovative method of providing care to patients.”
Dr. Dina Elrashidy
BOARD-CERTIFIED DERMATOLOGIST
Dr. Dina Elrashidy is a Clinical Instructor at Northwestern Memorial Hospital Department of Dermatology and a Dermatologist at the Northwestern Medical Group in Chicago. Dr. Elrashidy studied medicine at Wayne State University School of Medicine and completed her Dermatology residency at Henry Ford Hospital in Detroit, where she was chief resident. She is a Fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology.
“I take pride in educating my patients and helping them achieve optimal skin health, and believe that Spruce makes the entire process of dermatologic evaluation and treatment as smooth as possible.”
Dr. Maria Robinson
BOARD-CERTIFIED DERMATOLOGIST
Dr. Maria Robinson studied medicine at Pennsylvania State University College of Medicine and completed her residency at Case Western Reserve University Department of Dermatology. Dr. Robinson is a Clinical Assistant Professor of Dermatology at New York University School of Medicine and practices at Dermatology Associated in Plymouth Meeting, Pennsylvania.
“I’m interested in telemedicine in general, and specifically in providing high-quality dermatologic care in a way that is accessible and convenient for patients.”
Dr. Jason Fung
BOARD-CERTIFIED DERMATOLOGIST
Dr. Jason Fung studied medicine at the University of Rochester School of Medicine and Dentistry and completed his residency at Washington University Division of Dermatology. Dr. Fung runs a private practice in the San Francisco Bay Area. He is a fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology.
“I'm excited to have the opportunity to make a mobile house call, and help you create a personalized acne treatment regimen.”
Dr. Divya Srivastava
BOARD-CERTIFIED DERMATOLOGIST

“I look forward to bringing quality and timely dermatological care that is convenient for my patients and their busy schedules.”
